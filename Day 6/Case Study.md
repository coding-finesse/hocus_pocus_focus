## Case Study: The Decoy Effect 

*The Problem:* 

A cinema sells Small popcorn for $3 and Large for $7. Most people buy the Small to save money. Revenue stalls. Find a fix.

---

Sol:

To play with numbers & margins introduce a medium range priced close to large say at $6 or $6.5. Most people will shift to the Large popcorns as 50 or 100 cents difference changes the entire value and may overlook small.

To make ppl choose premium give an almost premium
