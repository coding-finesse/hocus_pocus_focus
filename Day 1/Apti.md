## Question

A shopkeeper offers a "Buy 2, Get 1 Free" deal.  
What is the actual percentage discount being offered to the customer?  
A) 25%  
B) 33.33%  
C) 50%  
D) 66.66%

**Answer:** B) 33.33%  
(Example: buying a ₹10 pen, you pay ₹20 for 3 pens worth ₹30; discount = ((30 - 20) / 30) × 100 ≈ 33.33%.)
