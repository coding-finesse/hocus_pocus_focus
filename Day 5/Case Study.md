## Case study 

A marketplace platform sees revenue up 25%, but profitability down.  
Take rate and traffic are flat, costs are higher.

What’s the first hypothesis you’d test and which 2–3 unit‑level metrics would you check?

---

Solution : 

Since revenue is up, but profitability is down (or losing money is evident), some mismatch in the business model must be causing higher costs or lower margins per unit.

Surprisingly users & fee earned as in commission both are constant.Still a loss is pointing to likely either AOV is too low to cover costs,or discounts are mis‑priced and burning cash per order. Also refunds/cancellations can add negatively too if it spiked & operational failure
