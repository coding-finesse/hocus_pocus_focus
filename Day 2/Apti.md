# Aptitude Challenge: The Two Ropes

**Problem:**
You have two ropes. Each takes exactly 60 minutes to burn from end to end.
The ropes are not uniform: some parts might burn fast, others slow. You can't just cut them in half to get 30 mins.

Using only these two ropes and a lighter, how do you measure exactly 45 mins?

**Solution:**
* **A🔥->30 mins<-🔥A** (burning rope A from both ends) ; to 45 mins (15 mins more using rope B by burning one end). 
* **B🔥->P1**(when A is burnt fully 30 mins) right then burn other end of B (to get 15 mins measured at P2).
* **B🔥->P1(30)->P2(15)🔥<-B**
