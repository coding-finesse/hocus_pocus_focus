### Apti

---

You have 10 bags full of gold coins. In 9 bags, every coin weighs 10g. In 1 bag, every coin weighs 9g. 

You have a digital scale, but you can only use it exactly once. How do you find the bag with the lighter coins?

---

**Ans :** 

10 bags say 2 coins each : 9 bags (1 coin->10g so 20g coins in 9 bags (180g) & 1 bag has 9g/coin so 18g in one bag). 

To find bag w lighter coins. Pick one coin from each bag 10 coins & weigh no it won’t tell which bag is lighter. Not the solvable one. Retry.

Take one coin from bag 1 , 2 from bag 2 , 3 from bag 3 … 10 from bag 10.Only 1 bag has 9g coins rest all 10g.The ideal weight should have been A.P. $(n \times (n+1)/2)$ (55 coins * 10g = 550g) but their weight would be say X. So 550g - Xg = Yg (light coins) Y will reveal bag number.
