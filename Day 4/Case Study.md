### Case study 

**Case: The "Free Shipping" Trap** 

**Client:** Z-Cart (E-comm)  
**Move:** Free Ship threshold $50 to $25.  
**Result:** Orders surged 30%, but Net Profit crashed.  
**Data:** AOV dropped $65 to $28. Flat $8 shipping/box.  
**Task:** Diagnose why profit died & give 1 fix.

---

**Analysis:**

Lowering the threshold (50->25) killed profits because the $8 shipping fee/box eats major revenue. AOV declined by $37, people are buying solo or cheap items instead of combo/expensive.

---

**Fix:** 
Don't just revert. Use threshold offers at checkout to AOV/ group high value add-ons.

Shipping is eating the profit (margin) not overall sales (low cost orders are responsible for that, it’s neither logistics nor marketing problem but unit economics was lacking)
