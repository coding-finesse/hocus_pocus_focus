### Case Study: The Growth Stagnation

**Scenario:**
An app has 10M daily active users, 2M paying users, and ₹200 avg revenue per paying user. User count grows but paying users don’t. What 2 non‑obvious reasons could explain this, which 1 metric would you track, and what 1 high‑impact change would you test first?

**Analysis:**
* **Current State:** 10M daily users, 2M paid, 8M not adding money
* **Revenue Calculation:** Avg revenue (2M * 200) rs ~ 400M rs/day
* **The Problem:** The 8M group (or more) is increasing, but there is no addition to the 2M paid group

---

**Point of View:**
* **Non-Obvious Reasons:**
    1. **Friction/Avoidance:** Curious users are getting stuck at the payment stage or are actively avoiding paid services
    2. **Audience Mismatch:** An economically weaker group or a mismatched audience is joining that does not see enough value in the current paid offerings

* **Metric to Track:**
    * Track clicks, subscriptions, and renewals

* **High-Impact Change to Test:**
    * Push targeted ads or pause some services
    * **Primary Test:** Offer a 1‑week free trial without a credit card wall for the non-paying tier
