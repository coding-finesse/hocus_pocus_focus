## Apti

Outside a room with 3 unlabeled bulbs sits a panel of 3 switches (one controls each bulb, but which is unknown). 

Manipulate switches freely beforehand, enter once to observe (no switch access inside). How determine each switch-bulb pair?

---

Sol:

Turn on 2 switches for 30 mins. Turn one off before entering. Enter room:

Bulb with light = switch left ON.

Warm (off but hot) = the switch turned OFF after 30 mins.

Cold & off = the untouched switch.

Hence mapped switches to respective bulbs
