## Case Study

Airline profits tanked. Why? Revenue = yield × seats filled. Costs? Fuel spiked 20%.

Fix: Surge pricing peaks, hedge fuel costs. Profits jump 15%

---

Sol:

Airline profits aren’t just about filling seats (Volume) but rather about margin on every ticket (Value)
If fuel spikes 20%:

Next fuel: Negotiate constant fuel rates ahead; 
Surge pricing: Raise last min fares to capture high value demand.
Focus on the margin over headcount

---

Better:

Airline profits = Value > Volume

If fuel spikes 20%, stop chasing headcount & start protecting margins:

1.Fuel Hedging: Lock in constant rates early to stabilize the floor

2.Surge Pricing: Hike last min fares to capture high val demand.

Revenue ->vanity, margin ->sanity.
